Reviews

Use this view to schedule, record, edit and view risk reviews. Each set of risks should undergoe a regular review process whereby the evaluation, controls and mitigation steps are revalidated, progress on mitigation steps monitored any incidences evaluated

Use the "Schedule New Review" button to schedule a new review

Once the review has been created, go to the Risk Explorer, select the risks to be reviewed and right click to assign them to the review

General review comments can be entered as well as specific comments against each risk

Selecting "Review Complete" prevents any further modification to the review
