Relative Matrix

The Relative Matrix allows to subjectively rate risk in respect to probability and impact relative to each other

Probability is on the vertical axis
Impact is on the horizonatl axis

  1. Select whether to display the risks in their Current, Treated or Un Treated state
     
  2. Click and Drag risks to their appropriate relative location
     
  3. Risks with identical Probability and Impact are shown offset to the right and below a bit so all risks are visible
     
  4. Use the Risk Owner and Risk Manager filters to focus on a set of risks
     
  5. Use the Locate Risk selector to highlight a risk on a crowded page
     
  6. The color of each risk title indicates wheter the risk is current Treated or Untreated
     
  7. Use the Project Navigator to select a different project.
     
  8. Details of the risk are displayed when the mouse passes over each item.
     
  9. Double Click on any item to open the Risk Editor for the item.
     
  10. Select Save to save any ranking changes.
     
  11. Select Reset to undo any changes since the last save.
