Incidents

Use the Incidents view to record incidents which are related to risks in the risk register

The Core Data tab is used to record the basic details of the incident and the effectiveness of the risk management controls put in place

The Objectives tab records the project objectives impacted by the incident

The Quantifiable Consequences tab is used to record the value of any quantifiable consequence that occurred as a result of the incident

The Risks Impacted tab is used to record the risks which are related to the incident.
Once the incident has been created, go to the Risk Explorer, select the impacted risks and right click to assign them to the incident

The Attachments tab can be used to attach any associated documents relating to the incident, such as derailed written reports, pictures, video etc.
