Summary Risks

Summary Risks allow you to group a number of related risks under one summary risk. This offers another form of classification in addition to the Primary and Secondary categories

  1. Drag risks and drop them on the appropriate Summary Risk
     
  2. Drag risks from either the Unallocated list or from other Summary Risks
     
  3. Use SHIFT to select multiple risks at once
     
  4. Use the button to create a new Summary Risk
     
  5. Use the Project Navigator to select the a different project.
